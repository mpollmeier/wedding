Schedule

The ceremony starts at 4pm. Please plan to arrive at the venue by 3.30pm at the latest, which means taking the ferry from Auckland at 2pm or earlier. The ceremony will be followed by ice cream, drinks, dinner and dancing until around midnight.

Transport

There are two ferry options from Auckland to Waiheke:
1. Fullers passenger ferry offers a discounted return ticket for $32 per person if you show a printout of the invitation email when you buy the ticket. They leave from the ferry terminal at Britomart and their last ferry back to Auckland leaves at 12.30am.

2. Sealink car and passenger ferry () offers a discounted return ticket for $... for one car including two passengers if you quote that you are guests of our wedding when you book. They leave from Halfmoon Bay or Wynyard Quarter in Auckland and go to Kennedy Point on Waiheke. Their last ferry back to Auckland leaves already at 7pm, so this is only an option if you choose to stay overnight.

For transport on the island, you can take a taxi to the venue and back to the ferry. It's a busy day with weddings on Waiheke and the taxis might book out, so please reserve the taxi as early as possible. These are some taxi companies on the island:
Island Taxis
Waiheke Express Taxis
Waiheke Independent Taxis


We do not recommend taking the local bus, as the times are unreliable and the venue is a 25 minute climb up a steep hill from the closest bus stop in Ostend.

Ceremony and reception will be in the backyard of a private home at 34 Seaview Road, about half-way between Ostend and Onetangi. There is some parking at the beginning of the driveway and more parking along Seaview Road. Please do not drive to the end of the driveway as you might not be able to turn around.

There is also an option for camping at the venue. Please let us know if you are interested in this in your RSVP.

Dress Code: Smart casual

The ceremony and ice cream will be in a grassy backyard, followed by the reception in a tent. We will be dressed-up, but we want our guests to feel free to wear whatever they are comfortable in.

Ten years of fun

Schedule

The ceremony will start at 4pm. Please arrive at the venue by 3.30pm at the latest, which means taking the ferry from Auckland at 2pm or earlier. The ceremony will be followed by ice cream, drinks, dinner and dancing until around midnight.

Transport

There are several options to get from Auckland to Waiheke Island, including:

1. Fullers passenger ferry offers a discounted return ticket for $32 per person if you show a printout of our wedding invitation email when you buy the ticket. They leave from the ferry terminal at Britomart and their last ferry back to Auckland leaves at 12.30am.

2. Sealink operates car and passenger ferries from Half Moon Bay and Wynyard Quarter to Kennedy Point. Their last ferry back to Auckland leaves already at 7pm, so this is only an option if you choose to stay overnight. Check for their discounts on bookme.co.nz.

For transport on the island, you can take a taxi to the venue and back to the ferry. There are many weddings on Waiheke on that day and the taxis might book out, so please reserve the taxi as early as possible. Here are some taxi companies on the island:
Island Taxis
Waiheke Express Taxis
Waiheke Independent Taxis

We do not recommend taking the local bus, as the times can be unreliable and the venue is a 25-minute climb up a steep hill from the closest bus stop in Ostend.

There is some parking at the beginning of the driveway. Please do not drive to the end of the driveway as you might not be able to turn around.

Dress Code

Smart casual. The ceremony and ice cream will be in a grassy backyard, followed by the reception in a tent with floor mats, so it's better to avoid stilettos.
